Research Focus

Operating at the intersection of "Dry Lab" computational biology and "Wet Lab" clinical validation.

Genomics & GWAS

Large-scale genome-wide association studies to identify susceptibility loci for Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE) in Asian populations.

AI Disease Prediction

Developing intelligent algorithms (e.g., Random Forests) to construct robust disease risk prediction models using genomic data.

Nanopore Sequencing

Application of Oxford Nanopore Third-Generation Sequencing (TGS) to analyze complex structural variants.
