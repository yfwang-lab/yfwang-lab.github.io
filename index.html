About

Our lab, based at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen School of Medicine, is dedicated to integrative multi-omics and AI-powered modeling to decipher the molecular mechanisms of complex diseases. We focus on immune-related and metabolic disorders such as systemic lupus erythematosus, diabetic myopathy, and various cancers including triple-negative breast cancer. By combining transcriptomics, genomics, and functional data with cutting-edge machine learning methods, our research aims to uncover novel biomarkers, regulatory interactions, and therapeutic targets. Recent efforts also explore population-specific genomic disparities and bioinformatics tools for clinical applications.
